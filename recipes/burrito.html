Burrito

Description

These quick and easy bang bang burritos feature breaded chicken strips, coleslaw mix, and a spicy 3-ingredient bang bang sauce. Wrapped in a flour tortilla and pan fried, they're hot, golden, and crunchy.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Breaded chicken strips
  • Coleslaw mix
  • Bang bang sauce

Instructions

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a skillet or microwave until pliable.
  2. Place a few breaded chicken strips on each tortilla.
  3. Add a generous amount of coleslaw mix on top of the chicken.
  4. Drizzle with bang bang sauce to taste.
  5. Roll up the tortillas tightly to enclose the filling.
  6. In a skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Place the burritos seam-side down and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side.
